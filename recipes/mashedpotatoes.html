Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons butter, divided

Step 1:

Place potatoes in a large pot. Cover with water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until potatoes are fork tender, about 30 minutes.

Step 2:

While potatoes are cooking, melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat. Remove from heat and stir in remaining 2 tablespoons butter. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3:

Whisk potatoes and butter mixture until smooth. Serve immediately.
